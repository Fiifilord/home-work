About Our STEM4ALL

We’re A Non-profit STEM4ALL Organization

At STEM4ALL, we address the low IT literacy among students, especially in deprived communities, by providing practical IT sessions. Our goal is to equip them with the necessary skills and spark their interest in STEM fields.

We offer a variety of programs, including mastering Microsoft Office, programming using Scratch, Git version control, problem-solving with LeetCode and HackerRank, and more. We also provide professional development training in LinkedIn profile creation, resume building, cover letter writing, and portfolio development.
